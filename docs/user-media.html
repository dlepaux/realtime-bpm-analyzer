If you do not have the alert, it means either that you already gave access to it or you have an issue with your material.
You can emulate the behaviour of a microphone by looping back your output as an input to your machine to detect BPM of what you're listening right now.
  • On Windows envrionment Enable the stereoMix in your audio params to emulate the behaviour of a micro.
  • On MacOS you can use LoopBack to put the output of Chrome on your micro input.
